Oil Change

We accept oil change to sedans & crossovers. Minivans may be accepted. SUVs and trucks are not accepted. Some European vehicles may not be accepted. Motor oil types include full synthetic, synthetic blend, conventional & high mileage. To contact us for quote & appointment (if necessary), please provide your vehicle's year, make, model and mileage (e.g. 2020 Honda Civic - 13,000km).

BYOFO - Bring your own filter and oil, and we can do the oil change for you. We will not do the oil change if you bring the incorrect engine oil and/or filter.

Complete Oil Change Kit - Not sure which oil and filter to get? We can get the oil filter and engine oil for you, prior to the oil change. You must contact us first, in order to confirm your appointment.
